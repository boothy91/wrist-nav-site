Map Screen

Controls and indicators

Location Button
Locked – map follows you
Free – moveable map
Compass
Locked North
Rotates with heading
  • Drag compass right to go back to stats pages
Map Lock
  • Tap the Clock/Battery bar and it will totally locks the map from motion, tap again and it will unlock
  • Drag compass right to return to stats
  • Map stays locked when you return to it
  • Small dot next to time & battery shows lock state
Map locked
Map free

Maps Page

Download and manage offline maps

Manage Maps
  • Tap to open already downloaded maps
  • Tap any map to delete it
Download Maps
  • Tap to open the download menu
  • Some countries have a single map file
  • Others use leaf maps — each region has its own map
  • Tap any region to download

Settings

All 23 options explained

1
Watch Theme
Visual style of the watch app
Fjord · Light · Dark
2
Map Theme
Colour style of the map tiles, including minimal variants and custom imported themes
Fjord · Light · Dark · All Minimal · Custom
3
Custom Edit
Activate, deactivate or delete imported custom map themes
4
Distance Unit
Affects all stats displays and lap split times
km · mi
5
Clock Format
12hr · 24hr
6
Rec Distance
Minimum movement before a GPS point is recorded
1m · 2m · 3m · 5m · 8m · 10m · 20m
7
Rec Seconds
Minimum time between recorded GPS points
1s · 2s · 3s · 5s · 8s · 10s · 20s
8
Import Colour
Colour of imported tracks when visible on the map
Red · Green · Yellow · Blue
9
Recording Colour
Colour of the track being recorded on the map
Red · Green · Yellow · Blue
10
Imported Track Width
Line thickness of imported tracks on the map
1px · 2px · 4px · 7px · 10px · 15px · 20px
11
Recording Track Width
Line thickness of the active recording track on the map
1px · 2px · 4px · 7px · 10px · 15px · 20px
12
Lap Splits
Vibrates and shows split time every set distance (reads Distance Unit setting)
On · Off
13
Lock Zoom
Sets the fixed zoom level when the location button is locked (red) on the map
14
Keep Active
Keeps the app active and prevents the watch from sleeping
15
Nav Distance
When Nav is on, shows remaining route distance on the home and map screens
On · Off
16
Nav Elevation
When Nav is on, shows elevation remaining and your position on the route profile
On · Off
17
Stats Edit
Customise the stats pages — up to 5 pages, choose which stats appear on each
18
Pace Average
Window used to calculate the rolling pace average
10s · 20s · 30s · 60s · All
19
Text Size
Size of text across the stats pages
Small · Medium · Large · Extra Large · Huge
20
Heart Rate
Enable or disable heart rate recording during a session
On · Off
21
Steps
Enable or disable step count recording during a session
On · Off
22
Countdown
Adds a countdown before recording starts
Off · 3s · 5s
23
Exit App
Fully close WristNav

Phone App

Companion app features

Send Tab
  • Send GPX files to your watch
  • Send .map files to your watch
  • Log in to Strava to sync activities
Received Tab
  • View files shared from your watch
  • Upload received files
  • Delete received files
Plan
  • Plan a route directly in the app
  • Save the route for later
  • Send the route to your watch
Theme
  • Create custom map themes for the watch
  • 10 presets to start from
  • Tap any element line to change its colour
Settings
  • Mirrors the watch settings — configure the app from your phone
