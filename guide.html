Home Screen

  • Tap the large circle button to start recording — a second tap goes straight to the map
  • GPS status indicator — red (no fix), green (good fix)
  • NAV OFF / NAV ON toggle at the bottom — tap to pick a GPX route to follow, tap again to clear it
  • When a route is loaded, starting recording goes straight into the map flow
  • Swipe right from Home to open the map directly
🗺️

Map Screen

Controls

  • Pinch to zoom, swipe to pan
  • Crown / rotary input for zoom on compatible watches, including OnePlus Watch 3 bezel support
  • Long press to open the main menu
  • Location button — red = locked to GPS position · green = unlocked and pan freely
  • Compass tape — tap to cycle modes · drag right to go back to stats
  • Map Lock — tap the clock/battery bar at the top to lock the map from all input · a small dot shows lock state · tap the dot to go back to Stats

Compass Modes

  • 🔴 Red — North locked: Map always faces north
  • 🟢 Green — Heading mode: Map rotates with your direction of travel
  • 🔵 Blue — Free compass: Map locked north, compass tape moves live with your bearing

Navigation Overlay

  • Remaining distance to end of route — toggle in Settings → Map → Map Settings → Nav Distance
  • Live elevation profile with your current position marked — toggle in Settings → Map → Map Settings → Nav Elevation

Bezel Stats

  • Live workout data curved along the bottom of round watch screens, straight bar on square watches
  • 3 configurable stat slots — enable in Settings → Map → Map Settings → Bezel Stats, then swipe right to configure slots
  • Text colour controlled by the Map Text colour setting
📥

Maps Page

Download Maps

  • Browse regions and download directly to the watch — no phone needed
  • Some countries have a single file, others use regional leaf maps
  • Tap any region to download
  • Available regions: boothy91.github.io/wrist-nav-maps
  • Swipe through downloaded maps — tap to delete
💡 You can also send .map files directly from the phone companion app.
🗂️

Tracks Page

Import GPX

  • Import GPX files sent from the phone companion app
  • Download OSM traces directly to the watch

Track List

  • Each track shows name and distance
  • Tap a track to open its detail view
  • Delete tracks from the detail view

Track Detail

  • Stats page: Distance, total time, moving time, date
  • Heart rate page: Avg, max and min HR (watch-recorded tracks only)
  • Cadence page: Avg cadence, max cadence, total steps (watch-recorded tracks only)
  • Elevation page: Gain, loss, max and min elevation
  • Graph page: Full elevation profile graph
📊

Stats Pages

Customisable Stats

  • Up to 10 pages, 3 stat slots per page
  • Optional stat labels — toggle via the phone companion app Settings tab
  • Configure in Settings → Stats → Stats Pages
  • Add and remove pages as needed

Recording

  • Recording continues when the screen sleeps via a background service
  • Vibrates at every km or mile split with split time shown on screen
  • Optional countdown timer before recording starts (3s or 5s)
  • Auto-pause when speed drops below a configurable threshold
  • Adjustable minimum GPS recording distance and time interval
  • Activity type written into GPX — Run, Trail Run, Walk, Hike, Road Cycle, Mountain Bike, Gravel, Kayak, Ski, Snowboard
  • GPX exported with timestamps, elevation, heart rate and cadence data
⌚

Watch Face Complications

Assign any WristNav stat to a complication slot on your watch face. Updates live during recording.
  • 15 complication providers — one per stat type
  • Select from your watch face customisation screen
  • Tap any complication to open WristNav
  • Shows live data during recording, placeholder when not recording

Phone Companion App

The watch app works fully standalone — the phone app is optional.

Send Tab

  • Send GPX route files directly to the watch
  • Send .map files to the watch for offline use
  • Log in to Strava to sync recorded activities

Received Tab

  • View GPX files recorded on the watch and transferred to your phone
  • Upload to Strava
  • Save to phone storage
  • Delete files

Plan Tab

  • Plan a route directly on the map with routed or straight-line segments
  • Load existing GPX files to view on the map
  • OSM, Topo and Satellite map styles
  • 3D terrain view
  • Export as GPX or send straight to the watch

Theme Tab

  • Create custom map themes for the watch
  • 10 colour presets to start from
  • Tap any element to change its colour with a full colour picker
  • Live map preview — see exactly what the theme looks like before sending
  • Send finished themes to the watch

Settings Tab

  • Phone app theme — Light / Fjord / Dark
  • Distance units — km / mi
  • Map style — Standard / Topo
  • Route colour and width
  • Stats labels — show or hide stat name labels on the watch stats pages
  • Auto delete received files — Off / 7 days / 30 days
